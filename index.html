Blue Jay

The blue jay.
The Cyanocitta cristata is a bird that is native to North America. They commonly reside in the eastern United States, as well as in Newfoundland, Canada.

Diet

Blue jays are considered omnivorous creatures, although their diet is mostly vegetable matter. The some common foods that they eat include:

  • Seeds
  • Peanuts
  • Acorns
  • Berries
  • Grain
  • Fruit
